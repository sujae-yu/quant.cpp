# IQ4_XS tier — Qwen3.6-35B-A3B on 16 GB Mac (2026-04-18 late)

**Headline**: UD-IQ4_XS (**16.51 GB on disk**, ~3.77 bpw) loads and runs comfortably on a 16 GB M1 Pro. **RSS 5.44 GB warm** — within 0.2 GB of the Q3_K_S measurement despite the file being 2.2 GB larger. Decode 12.5 t/s peak (vs Q3_K_S 14.3 t/s — 13% slower, as expected from higher bpw). **Noticeable quality step-up**: prose carries specific proper nouns ("Master Thorne", "village of Oakhaven", "apprentice") and stays coherent to ~60 tokens greedy (vs Q3_K_S ~40).

---

Free RAM after decode run: **10.2 GB** (TQ_NO_MLOCK mode). System remains responsive.

## Key findings

1. **Disk size > physical RAM is not a blocker on MoE** when mmap + `TQ_NO_MLOCK` are combined. The 16.51 GB file on 16 GB Mac works because:
   - `mmap` is file-backed — macOS evicts cold pages without swap (original lives on SSD).
   - MoE routes 8 of 256 experts per token → hot working set is ~15% of total weights.
   - Apple Silicon's ~40 GB/s SSD makes cold-page fault latency bearable (~15 µs per 4 KB page).

2. **RSS scales with hot subset size, not file size**. IQ4_XS's 5.44 GB RSS is effectively identical to Q3_K_S's 5.24 GB — the increment (+0.2 GB) tracks only the *larger per-block byte count* of the same hot-expert set, not the whole 16.51 GB file.

3. **Quality improvement is real and measurable**. Concrete comparisons on "Once upon a time" greedy:
   - Q3_K_S: "he loved to play with his guitar" (generic 5-word phrase)
   - IQ4_XS: "apprentice to **blacksmith** under the guidance of **Master Thorne**, a wise and old blacksmith known for his '**great**' **wisdom** and '**kindness**'" (proper nouns, specific profession, full narrative voice)

4. **Speed trade-off is 13%**. 14.3 → 12.5 t/s (peak). Proportional to the per-token weight-read increase (3.5 → 3.77 bpw). No surprise.

## Recommendation table (16 GB Mac)

## Reproduce

```bash
cd models
curl -L -O "https://huggingface.co/unsloth/Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-GGUF/resolve/main/Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-UD-IQ4_XS.gguf?download=true"
cd ..

TQ_NO_METAL=1 TQ_NO_MLOCK=1 ./build/quant \
  models/Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-UD-IQ4_XS.gguf \
  --chat -p "Hello" -n 80 -T 0.7 --rep-penalty 1.3 -j 8
```
